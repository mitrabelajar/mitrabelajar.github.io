4. apa itu was dan were​


Was maupun were adalah past tense (bentuk lampau) dari kata kerja to be. “Were” berkaitan dengan pengandaian dan “was” merupakan kebalikan darinya yaitu fakta.

17. penggunaan kata was, was not, were, were not​


Jawaban:

- was

I was doing my homework.

- was not

I was not there during that time.

They were chasing each other in the field.

We were having a fun time on the beach.

- were not

We were not doing practice yesterday.

You were not able to get to class in time.

Penjelasan:

Was digunakan untuk first person singular (orang pertama tunggal) – I, dan third person singular (orang ketiga tunggal) – he, she, it. Were digunakan untuk second person singular and plural – you, your, yours – juga first and third person plural (orang pertama ) – we , they. Hope this helps :)
