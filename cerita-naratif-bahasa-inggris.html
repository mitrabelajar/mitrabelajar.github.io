The Legend of Toba Lake and Samosir Island

Once upon a time, there was a good man who was living in north of Sumatra. He lived in a small house near the forest. Every day he farmed and fished to meet his daily needs

One day, he caught a golden fish by his trap. It was the big golden fish, biggest catch which he ever had in his life.

Miraculously, the golden fish can turn into a beautiful princess. The man fell in love seeing the princess’s beauty. So he proposed.

The princess replied, “I want to be your wife, but on the condition that you may not tell this secret to anyone. If you break it, then there will be a flood and I will become a fish again.”

The man made an agreement and finally they got married. They live happily and have a daughter.

A few years later, the girl intends to deliver lunch to her father in the fields. However, because of the slippery road, the food was spilled.

Knowing that, the father became angry. The man snapped at his daughter, ” You damned daughter of a fish!”

Feeling sad, the girl came home and asked her mother. The mother was disappointed because her husband did not keep his promise.

Then, the princess incarnated the fish telling her daughter to run up the hill. When his daughter arrived on the hill, the incarnate daughter of the fish prayed for a rainstorm.

Then the area was flooded. The whole land was submerged in water, except for the hill that was visited by girls. The flooded area is known as Danau Toba, while the hill visited by the girl is Samosir Island.

Legenda Danau Toba dan Pulau Samosir

Suatu ketika, ada seorang pria baik yang tinggal di bagian utara Sumatera. Dia tinggal di sebuah rumah kecil di dekat hutan. Setiap hari ia bertani dan memancing untuk memenuhi kebutuhan sehari-hari

Pria itu membuat setuju dan akhirnya mereka menikah. Mereka hidup bahagia dan memiliki seorang anak perempuan.

Beberapa tahun kemudian, anak perempuan itu berniat untuk mengantarkan makan siang untuk ayahnya di ladang. Namun, karena jalan yang licin, bekal makanan itu tumpah.

Mengetahui itu, sang ayah menjadi marah. Pria itu membentak anak perempuannya, “Anak perempuan ikan!”

Karena merasa sedih, anak perempuan itu pulang dan bertanya kepada ibunya. Sang ibu kecewa karena suaminya tidak menepati janji.

Lalu, putri jelmaan ikan itu menyuruh putrinya berlari ke atas bukit. Ketika anak perempuannya sudah sampai di atas bukit, putri jelmaan ikan itu berdoa meminta hujan badai.

Maka daerah itu terendam banjir. Seluruh tanah itu terendam air, kecuali bukit yang disinggahi oleh anak perempuan. Wilayah yang banjir tersebut dikenal dengan nama danau Toba, sementara bukit yang disinggahi anak perempuan itu adalah pulau Samosir.
