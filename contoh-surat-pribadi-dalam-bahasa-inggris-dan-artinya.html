5. Contoh surat pribadi untuk teman bahasa inggris dan artinya menanyakn kabar,dan strukturnya


Jawaban:

Dear Tomo,

Assalamu'alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh.

Hello my friend, how are you and your family? I hope you all are well there. Sorry, I haven't had the chance to ask how you are there, because my daily schedule is really busy. Here I am lonely Tomo, because Billy is away with his family, but I do not know where they are going and for what purpose, maybe they are on vacation in the village.

And I really miss your sister Tasya, I will send her a set of unicorn clothes, and if you want to reply to my letter, please insert the photo. Also, I really miss your Mother's tart, it really impressed my tongue. Longing greetings from me and my family. See you next month!

From your best friend, Muhammad.

Terjemahan:

Dear Tomo,

Assalamu'alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh.

Halo sahabatku, apa kabar mu dan keluargamu? Ku harap kalian baik - baik saja disana. Maaf, aku belum sempat menanyakan kabar mu di sana, karena jadwal harian ku sungguh padat. Disini aku kesepian Tomo, karena Billy sedang pergi bersama keluarga nya, namun aku tidak tahu mereka akan kemana dan bertujuan apa, mungkin mereka berlibur ke desa.

Dan aku sungguh rindu sekali dengan adik mu Tasya, akan aku kirimkan dia satu setel baju unicorn, dan jika kau mau membalas surat ku ini, selipkan foto nya ya. Juga, aku sangat rindu dengan kue tart buatan Bunda mu, itu sangat berkesan untuk lidah ku. Salam rindu dari ku dan keluargaku. Sampai ketemu bulan depan!

Dari sahabatmu, Muhammad.

Struktur :

• Receiver (Penerima surat)

• Main sentence (Kalimat utama : Isi / tujuan utama)

• Sender (Pengirim).

10. Contoh surat pribadi tentang berita buruk dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Condolence Letter

Dear Alicia,


It was with a great sense of loss when I heard of Jenny's death. I wanted to let you know that my heart is truly saddened.

Jenny was more than just a wonderful person; she was always so kind and considerate to us that we always welcomed seeing her at every opportunity.

Her passing will not only leave a void in our lives, but in the hearts of all those who knew her. Jenny will always remain within my heart

If there is anything that I can do to help you in anyway, please do not hesitate to call me at anytime. You can phone me at ( area code and phone number) to talk or if you need my help with anything.


My sincere thoughts and prayers are with you.


With deepest sympathy,


Bella

Pembahasan

Salah satu contoh surat pribadi tentang berita buruk adalah condolence letter (surat bela sungkawa).

Condolence letter written to show sympathy, usually for the recipient. You might wish them strength through their grief, say your fond memories of the person they have lost.
