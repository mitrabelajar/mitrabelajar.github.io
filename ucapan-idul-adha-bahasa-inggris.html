5. cerita tentang idul adha pke bahasa inggris


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: narrative
Pembahasan:

About a few years ago, in my old neighbourhood, there had always been a celebration of Ied Adha with its cattle slaughtering too. It was run by the neighbourhood community and done by almost all people there together after having Ied prayer in the early morning at 6 am. The male would be dealing with slaughtering and cutting the meat, while the female would be responsible for the meat distribution and cooking. Back then, there were 1 cow and 7 goats, which are quite many to handle in a small neighbourhood. Yet, the people were all happy helping one another while doing it. The whole process of slaughtering to the distribution took about four hours and eventually the meat were given to those who are in need and the entire people there as well.

7. karangan hari raya idul adha dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya


Eid Adha


Every year, muslims not only celebrate Eid Fitr as the Big Day. There is another Big Day called Eid Adha. Eid Adha is a little bit different with Eid Fitr. In Eid Fitr we usually eat Ketupat or Coconut Leaf Wrapped Rice Cake and Opor Ayam or Chicken in Spicy Coconut Gravy, but in Eid Adha we have to slaughter goat, sheep or cow.

The slaughter party of goat, sheep or cow is actually inspired by the history of Prophet Ibrahim who willingly sacrifice Prophet Ismail (his son) to be slaughtered but by Allah's Will his son turned to be a sheep.

The meat not only bought back by the person who buy but also all people around them. Usually those poor and unlucky people get some part of the meat from the owner.

Eid Adha taught muslims to share their belongings to everyone especially to those who are unlucky and poor.

Eid al-Adha

Eid al-Adha (Arabic: عید الاضحیٰ‎, romanized: Eid 'al 'Adha, is a  'Religious Festival of the Sacrifice') is the latter of the two official holidays celebrated within Islam. The Festival honors the willingness of Ibrahim (Abraham) to sacrifice his son Ismail as an act of obedience to Allah's command. Before Abraham could sacrifice his son, however, Allah provided a lamb to sacrifice instead. In commemoration of this intervention, animals are sacrificed ritually. One third of their meat is consumed by the family offering the sacrifice, while the rest is distributed to the poor and needy. Thank you.

Penjelasan:

Iedul Adha

Iedul Adha (Bahasa Arab: الاضحیٰ‎, diromanisasi: Iedul Adha, adalah sebuah 'Festival Religius Kurban') adalah yang ke-dua dari dua hari raya resmi yang dirayakan dalam Islam. Festival ini menghormati kesediaan Ibrahim (Abraham) untuk mengorbankan putranya Ismail sebagai tindakan ketaatan pada perintah Allah. Namun, sebelum Ibrahim bisa mengorbankan putranya, Allah menyediakan seekor domba untuk dikorbankan. Untuk memperingati intervensi ini, hewan dikorbankan secara ritual. Sepertiga dari daging mereka dikonsumsi oleh keluarga yang mempersembahkan kurban, sedangkan sisanya dibagikan kepada fakir miskin dan orang yang membutuhkan. Terima kasih.

20. karangan tentang hari raya idul adha dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Karangan tentang Hari Raya Idul Adha dalam Bahasa Inggris

Last Sunday I celebrated Eid al-Adha with my family. A week before Eid, my father handed over the goat that he bought to be slaughtered as a sacrificial animal. Then we also fasted because it is recommended for Muslims to fast two days before Eid al-Adha.

On Sunday morning we performed the Eid al-Adha prayer at the mosque. We met the villagers and shook hands with them and offered our greetings and prayers for them. After talking with the villagers we returned home to eat the food that my mother had prepared the day before. My mother cooked ketupat and chicken curry. I really like my mom's cooking because it tastes good, and it's even better when I eat it with my family.

After eating, we went back to the mosque to watch the slaughtering of the sacrificial animals. The time came when my father's goat was to be slaughtered. We watched with joy and with the hope that the meat could benefit others. After slaughtering, the animals are skinned so that the meat can be shared. My mom and dad helped the villagers cut the meat, and I helped take pictures and record the event. After being weighed, the meat is then handed over to those in need. I was happy to participate in this year's Eid al-Adha celebration.

Pembahasan

Terjemahan karangan tentang Hari Raya Idul Adha

Hari minggu lalu saya merayakan hari raya Idul Adha bersama keluarga saya. Seminggu sebelum lebaran, Ayah saya menyerahkan kambing yang ia beli yang akan disembelih sebagai hewan kurban. Lalu kami juga berpuasa karena disunahkan bagi kaum muslim untuk berpuasa dua hari sebelum hari raya Idul Adha.

Pada minggu pagi kami melaksanakan sholat Idul Adha di masjid. Kami bertemu dengan warga dan berjabat tangan seraya memberikan ucapan dan doa untuk mereka. Setelah berbincang dengan warga kami pun kembali ke rumah untuk menyantap masakan yang telah disiapkan Ibu saya sehari sebelumnya. Ibu saya memasak ketupat dan kari ayam. Saya sangat menyukai masakan Ibu karena rasanya enak, dan lebih enak lagi jika saya menyantapnya bersama keluarga.

Setelah makan, kami sekeluarga kembali ke masjid untuk menyaksikan penyembelihan hewan kurban. Tibalah saatnya ketika kambing Ayah saya akan disembelih. Kami menyaksikannya dengan suka cita dan dengan harapan bahwa dagingnya dapat bermanfaat bagi orang lain. Setelah disembelih, hewan-hewan tersebut kemudian dikuliti agar daging nya bisa dibagi. Ayah dan Ibu saya membantu warga memotong daging yang akan diberikan, dan saya membantu mengambil gambar dan merekam kegiatan tersebut. Setelah ditimbang, kemudian daging-daging tersebut akan diserahkan kepada orang yang membutuhkan. Saya senang bisa berpartisipasi dalam perayaan Idul Adha tahun ini.

Karangan diatas merupakan contoh dari recount text. Recount text merupakan jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang menceritakan pengalaman penulis. Tujuan penulisan recount text yakni memberikan informasi tentang pengalaman penulis sekaligus menghibur pembaca.

Dalam penulisan recount text terdapat generic structure sebagai berikut:

Orientation, berisi informasi umum mengenai tokoh, tempat kejadian, kapan kejadian berlangsung dan lain sebagainya.Events, merupakan isi dari teks berupa cerita mengenai pengalaman yang ingin disampaikan oleh penulis kepada pembaca.Reorientation, merupakan bagian penutup yang berisi kesimpulan dari kegiatan penulis.
