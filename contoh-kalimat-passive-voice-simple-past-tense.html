9. Rumus kalimat passive voice dalam bentuk simple present tense,simple past tense, simple future tense.


Simple Present Tense

Aktif : S + do/does + V1

Pasif : S + to be (am, are, is) + V3 + by + O

Contoh:

Aktif : Students speak English

Pasif : English is spoken by students

Simple Past Tense

Aktif : S + V2 + O

Pasif : S + to be (was, were) + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : Students spoke English

Pasif : English was spoken by students

Passive voice

1. simple present tense

S (direct object) + is/am/are + past participle +/- by … (agent)

2. simple past tense

S (direct object) + was/were + past participle +/- by … (agent)

3. simple future tense

S (direct object) +will + be + past participle +/- by … (agent)

14. Apakah kalimat di bawah ini merupakan kalimat Simple Past Tense (Passive Voice) ?? "Myself was introduced by me"


JAWABAN

Ya, kalimat tersebut termasuk dalam kalimat pasif Simple Past Tense. Mengapa?

____________PENJELASAN

Bentuk aktif dari kalimat tersebut adalah:

I introduced myself

kalimat tersebut bila diubah kembali menjadi kalimat pasif bentuknya menjadi:

Myself was introduced by me

Rumus kalimat pasif Simple Past Tense adalah:

Subject + was/were + Verb 3 + by + Agent

Perhatikan bahwa kalimat pasifnya sudah sesuai dengan rumus yang ada.

Dengan demikian dapat kita simpulkan bahwa kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat pasif Simple Past Tense.

Apakah kalimat "Myself was introduced by me" merupakan kalimat Simple Past Tense (Passive Voice)?

Answer:

Bukan

"Myself was introduced by me" bukan merupakan kalimat pasif.

Jika kalimat tersebut diubah dalam konstruksi active menjadi:

"I introduced myself" bukan dalam active voice, sehingga tidak bisa dijadikan passive.

"I introduced myself" merupakan kalimat dalam middle voice.

Middle voice adalah kalimat yang berkonstruksi seperti kalimat aktif (Agent + V + Patient) tetapi memiliki arti pasif karena subjek dan objek kalimat sama-sama berperan sebagai agent (pelaku) dan patient (penderita).

Middle voice adalah satu dari tiga grammatical voice.

Ciri middle voice adalah objek kalimat berupa reflexive pronoun.

Middle voice dapat menggunakan transitive maupun intransitive voice.

Kadang-kadang middle voice tidak menggunakan objek.

Verb pada middle voice selalu mengenai subjek seperti pada passive voice.

Contoh kalimat dalam middle voice:

I shaves myself every morning.

I shaves every morning. (the 'myself' is implied).

The window broke.

Perlu diketahui bahwa terdapat 5 Non Convertible Tenses, artinya terdapat 5 bentuk kalimat yang tidak bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive Voice:

Present Perfect Continuous TensePast Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Tense

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat.

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat.

Active Voice:

1. Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

2. Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Object

3. Present Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

4. Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

5. Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-ing + Object

6. Past Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Had + Verb-3 + Object

7. Simple Future Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object

8. Future Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Be + Verb-ing + Object

9. Future Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Have + Verb-3 + Object

Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3
