12² + 13² - 4!

= 12² + 13² - 4!

= 313 - 24

289

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

= 12² + 13² - 4!

= ( 12 × 12 ) + 13² - 4!

= 144 + 13² - 4!

= 144 + ( 13 × 13 ) - 4!

= 144 + 169 - 4!

= 144 + 169 - ( 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 )

= 144 + 169 - ( 12 × 2 × 1 )

= 144 + 169 - ( 24 × 1 )

= 144 + 169 - 24

= 313 - 24

= 289
