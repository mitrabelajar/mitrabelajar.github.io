14. biografi SINGKAT tentang tokoh terkenal dalam bahasa inggris.


Jawaban:

biografi tokoh Tere Liye

Tere Liye is a well-known writer in Indonesia. The name Tere Liye is the pen name of a writer named Darwis. Tere Liye is arguably the most prolific writer today. Because this writer always releases new works every year. Tere Liye was born in the interior of Sumatra on May 21, 1979. Currently, he is about to turn 40 years old.

The author of this novel is a writer who has very unusual novel ideas. Almost all novel genres have been written by Tere Liye. He has written novels on various themes ranging from love, politics, economics, religion, education and various other themes. Every time Tere Liye releases a novel, the novel published by him is always being hunted by Indonesian novel lovers.

Even if you look at Tere Liye's background, he doesn't have a trace of a writer. His education is in Economics from the University of Indonesia campus. Tere Liye's father and mother were just ordinary farmers. However, Tere Liye's talent in writing is not in doubt. Even some of his novels have been filmed. Some of the works that were filmed were Hafalan sholat Delisa and Bidadari-bidadari surga.

What makes many people even more amazed is that writing is not Tere Liye's main job. Tere Liye is an employee at a private company in Indonesia. Writing is just a hobby of Tere Liye. Through writing, readers can learn many things. In the novel Negeri Para Bedebah, readers can learn about world economy and politics. In the novel Sunset bersama Rossi, readers can learn about the sincerity of true love.

When reading Tere Liye's works, the author will be presented with an unusual story. From all these stories the reader will understand that the author of this novel has a very broad knowledge of the outside world. Currently, Tere Liye has written more than 30 novels. Of course this is a very impressive achievement.
