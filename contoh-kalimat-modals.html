1. Contoh kata modals dan conyoh kalimat menggunakan modals !!!


1. can/could (menyatakan kemampuan/kesanggupan, meminta izin)
-she can sing a song beautifully 
-i can speak arabic
-can i rrow your pencil?
-she could finih her job yesterday
-could you take that newpaper for me?
2. may/might (menyatakan permohonan izin, harapan)
- may i borrow your book?
-may God bless you, futaba
-may you both be happy
-she might be late yesterday
-you might try to be more careful
3. must/have to (menyatakan keharusan)
-you must go home now
-he must study hard
-we have to go now
4. shall/will (menyatakan perkiraan, menawarkan bantuan)
-i shall go to kupang tomorrow
-shall i open the door?
- will you accompany me?
5. should (menyatakan anjuran, bentuk lampaunya dri shall)
-you should study hard
- i should speak english yesterday 
- if you are ill you should go to the doctor soon
6. would (menyatakan permohonan yg sopan, bentuk lampau dri will)
-would you please help me?
- she would go here yesterday
7. dll (masih banyak lagi penjelasannya ini saja dulu kali yah)

15. berikan 5 contoh modals dari masing-masing modals


have,
has,
do,
does,           
was,
present: can, may, will, shall, must, have to. -> to be able to "is/am/are"
past: should, could, would, must, had to. -> to be able to "was/were"

rumus: (+) s + modals + v1/be
(-) s + modals + not + v1/be.
(?) modals + v1/be.?
cttan: "be" digunakan jika suatu kalimat menggunakan adj, noun, dll. dana bila suatu kalimat menggunakan kata kerja maka gunakan verb 1 (tnpa be)
contoh: - i will fly (saya akan terbang)
- i will be here (saya akan disini)
