5. contoh 2 percakapan tentang asking dan giving attention​


Jawaban:

1. Situation : The students and the teacher are in the classroom

Teacher : Attention, please.

Students : Yes, Sir.

Teacher :  I’m going to give you the itinerary of our journey.

Students :  Hurray!(noise)

Teacher :  May I have your attention, please!

Student A :  Be quiet, please!

Teacher :  Thank you. Here is the itinerary. One for each of you.

Student A :  Thank you, Sir.

2. Sara : Will you hear my story, Rik?

   Roki : Yes, I will. What’s about?

   Sara : It is about our friend Dina. Listen to me!

   Roki : Okay!

   Sara : Dina asked me to join her to Bali next Sunday.

   Roki : And then?

   Sara : I confused because next week I will have my first test in my course.

   Roki : I see. Then?

   Sara : How to say to Dina if I can’t join her?

   Roki : Just say the trust.

   Sara : Okay I will try.

   Roki : Look at me!

   Sara : Why?

   Roki : What do you think about this book?

   Sara : Wow! That’s a good book. Next time I will borrow it with you.

   Roki : Yes, sure.

Penjelasan:

Asking attention:

-Attention please! = Tolong perhatiannya!

-Can I get the attention = Bisakah saya mendapatkan perhatiannya

-Can I get the attention! Thanks = Bisakah saya mendapatkan perhatiannya! Terima kasih

-Can I have your attention, please? = Bisakah saya minta perhatian Kalian?

-Everybody, may I have your attention, please ? = Semua orang, boleh saya minta perhatiannya?

-Excuse me! = Permisi!

-Excuse me, I wonder if I could trouble you  = Maaf, Saya mau tanya bisakah Anda membantu

Giving attention:

-Ok. = Baik.

-OK. I See you = BAIK. Saya melihat Anda

-Really? = Betulkah?

-Tell me more about it. = Ceritakan lebih banyak tentang itu.

-That’s alright = Itu benar

-Well, well, well… Oh, no! = Yah, well, well … Oh, tidak!

-What happens next? = Apa yang terjadi selanjutnya?

-What’s next? = Apa berikutnya?

-Yes Sir/Ma’am = Ya, Sir / Nyonya

-Yes sir/mam/madam = Ya, tuan / nyonya / nyonya
