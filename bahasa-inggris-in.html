7. apa yang dimaksud dengan in dalam bahasa Inggris


dalam bahasa inggris in artinya di dalamIn artinya : Di / di dalam
Arti "in" dalam bahasa Inggris berbeda, tergantung dengan kalimatnya. Contoh :

My pencil is "in" my bag. = Pensilku ada di "dalam' tasku.

I live "in' Jakarta. = Aku tinggal "di" Jakarta.

Those sentences are called proverbs, which is expression in metaphor to state a wisdom or advice in an implicit way but tends to be quite effective as those words stays in our memory. 

a. The meaning of 'time flies when you're having fun' is that you don't feel the time at all when you have fun, it feels short though actually the time is still there, not flying, but running in a steady speed.

18. bahasa inggris in, dan bahasa negatif nya!


1. she read the novel last night

(-) she didnt read the novel last night

2. we went to Bali last month

(-) we didnt go to Bali last month

1. She read a novel last night.

(Negative) She didn't read a novel last night.

2. We went to Bali last month.

(Negative) We didn't go to Bali last month.

--------------------------------------------------------

Penjelasan:

Dalam kalimat positif simple past tense, kata kerja yang digunakan adalah kata kerja bentuk kedua (verb 2).

Dalam kalimat negatifnya, ada penambahan kata kerja bantu "didn't" sebelum menulis kata kerja bentuk pertama (verb 1).
