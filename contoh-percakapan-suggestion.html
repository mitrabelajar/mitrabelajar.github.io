10. Contoh percakapan " suggestion and offer " ??


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: suggestion and offer
Kata kunci: suggestion and offer
Pembahasan:

Offer is  offering help to others. Example: shall I/ would you like me to help you?
suggestion is giving an idea to others. Example: shall/ why don't we play chess?

Dialogue:
R:Jose, what shall we do this weekend?
J: I haven't got any idea, Rania.
R: Come on, honey, think of something. I don't want to go cycling again.
J: What about it?
R: It's boring. I'd rather do something else.
J: OK, let's go to the beach. There's a party there.
R: Really? I'm surprised you don't know that I hate parties.
J: Sorry, dear, I forget. Ah, why don't we go to the book fair? It's just opened yesterday.
R: That'll be a better idea. What time shall we go?
J: Shall we leave at 10 am?
R: OK, hon. Shall I make your favourite cake for our snack on the way?
J: Thanks, honey. That's very sweet of you. I'll pick you up at 9.30 am, okay?
R: Sure, I won't be going anywhere. 



11. Contoh percakapan antara compliant and suggestion


Waiter: Good evening sir! Welcome to Simpang Tigo Restaurant. This is our menu, you can choose the food or drink you like. (Selamat malam pak! Selamat datang di restoran SIMPANG TIGO. Inu menu kami pak, Anda dapat memilih minuman dan makanan yang anda sukai.)

Customer: Thank you. What is the best seller here? (Terima kasih. Menu apa yang paling laris disini?)

Waiter : We have the recommended drink for you sir. It’s avocado and dragon fruit juice. For the foor of course Rendang. What do you want to order, Sir? (Kami punya minuman terbaik untuk anda pak. Jus alpukan dan buah naga. Untuk makanannya tentu saja Rendang. Anda mau pesan yang mana, Pak?)

Customer: I am interested in the juice. Give me one, but please you don’t need to give dragon fruit and give extra sugar please. Wait.. and Rendang with cincang soup for the food. (Aku tertarik dengan jusnya. Beri aku satu, tapi tolong anda tidak perlu menambahkan buah naganya dan tolong berikan juga ekstra gula. Tunggu untuk makanannya rendang dengan sup cincangnya.)

Waiter: Okay. Please wait for about five minutes. (Oke. Mohon tunggu sekitar lima menit.)

Cutomer: It’s okay. (Tidak masalah.)

10 minutes later……… (10 Menit kemudian………)

Waiter: These are your orders sir. A glass of avocado and dragon fruit juice and Rendang with Cincang soup. (Ini semua pesanan anda pak. Segelan jus alpukat dan buah naga dan rending dengan sup cinang nya.)

Cutomer: Excuse me! I am sorry but this is not like what I expected. (Maaf! Saya mohon maaf, tapi ini tidak seperti apa yang aku harapkan.)

Waiter: I’m so sorry sir. Is there any problem with your order? (Maaf pak. Apakah ada yang salah dengan pesanannya?)

Customer: Yeah. I think there is something wrong about the juice. Do you still remember what I’ve ordered? (Ya. Saya pikir ada yang salah dengan jusnya. Apakah kamu masih ingat dengan apa yang telah aku pesan?)

Waiter: Okay sir, let me repeat your order. A galss avocado and dragon fruit juice and Rendang with Cincang soup. (Oke pak, aku akan ulangi pesanan anda. Segelas jus alpukat dan buah naga dan Rendang dengan sup cincang.)

Customer: Well, I’ve just ordered a glass avocado juice without dragon fruit and add some extra sugar. (Baikalah, aku baru saja memesan segelas jus alpukat tanpa buah naga dan tolong berikan ekstra gula.)

Waiter: Oh.. I am so sorry sir. That’s all my responsibility. Please forgive me, I will change the juice. (Oh… saya minta maaf pak. Semuanya tanggung jawab saya. Mohon maaaf kan saya, saya akan mengganti jus nya.)

Customer: It’s all right. (Gak papa.)

Waiter: It’s your new order sir. I hope your are enjoy eating here. (Ini pesanan baru anda pak. Saya harap anda menikmati makan disini.)

Customer:Thanks, you have the best and quick delivery service. (Terima kasih, restoran anda mempunyai pelayanan yang terbaik dan tercepat.)

Waiter: That is our priority sir. Anything else, sir? (Itu adalah prioritaskami pak. Ada lagi yang lainnya?)

Customer: No, thank you. (Tidak, terima kasih.)

Waiter : Well. Have a nice dinner, sir. (Baiklah. Semoga makan malamnya nikmat pak.)

Customer: Sure. (Tentu.)

19. Contoh percakapan 4 orang Inggris tentang suggestion


Here's the conversation among four people about suggestion:

Ron: Hi, Paul, what shall we do this weekend?

Paul: Hmm, I have no idea. Hey, let's ask Matt here.

Matt: Hey, mates, what are you doing here?

Ron: Well, we were just talking about the weekend plan?

P: You got any idea, Matt?

M: Hmm, how about playing bowling?

R: Not that, please. Don't you have any other ideas?

P: Yeah, bowling is kinda dull.

M: Hey, chill out, would you? You asked for my ideas. That's from me.

Gareth: Fellows, what are you up to?

P: Ah, we have Gareth here. Let's ask him.

M: Yeah, we're planning something for the weekend, care to share any thoughts? We haven't got any yet.

G: Let's go fishing.

P: No, I'd rather go to library then. Fishing makes me drowsy.

G: Ah, what about that? Going to the library?

M: What's in there? Any events?

G: Don't you read any pamphlets? There's a garage sale for various books in the City Library, plus book signing events from several authors, one of them is Phillip Pullman.

R: What? No kidding? The Phillip Pullman?

P: Yup, he's right. Look here, I just checked the Library's Instagram.

M: Well done, Paul.

G: Yeah, what a way to suggest an idea.

R: Nah, he's just lucky. So, when shall we meet?

P: I'd say, why don't we meet at 9.30? so, not that crowded yet.

M: Couldn't agree more.

G: Good idea.

Penjelasan:

Suggestion is a way to give an idea on something. The question for it is

what shall we do? / where shall we go? Shall we .....?Why don't we....?Let's .....How/ what about + Ving?

The best response can be like

sure, good idea (for accepting it)sorry, I'd rather do something else. (for refusing it)I'm afraid I can't do that. Can we do something else. (for refusing it)
