V = 1/3 × 3,14 × (18)² × 26

V = 8.817,12 cm³

`Geometry

Diketahui :

jari-jari (r) = 18 cmgaris pelukis (s) = 30 cm

Ditanya :

Volume kerucut

Dijawab :

Cari tinggi kerucut

t² = s² - r²

t² = 30² - 18²

t² = (30×30) - (18×18)

t² = 900 - 324

t² = 576

t = √576

t = √24²

t = 24 cm

Volume kerucut

V = 1/3 × π × r² × t

V = 1/3 × 3,14 × 18² × 24

V = 1/3 × 3,14 × (18 × 18) × 24

V = 1/3 × 3,14 × 324 × 24

V = 1 × 3,14 × 108 × 24

V = 8.138,88 cm³
