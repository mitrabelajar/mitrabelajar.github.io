Nanotechnology

What is nanotechnology? Technology is the activity or study of using scientific knowledge for practical purposes in industry, farming, medicine, business etc. Hence, nanotechnology means the study of the scientific application of nanoparticles. Nanoparticles are very, very small in size and they are measured in terms of nanometres (nm). A nanometre is one-billionth of a metre.  

To visualise its size let us go down from the length of a metre. We all know what a metrelength is. One tenth of this metre is a decimeter. One hundredth of a metre is a centimetre (cm). A millimetre is (mm) one thousandth of a metre. A micro-metre is one millionth of a metre. Allthese are still huge compared to a nanometre. A nanometre is one-billionth of a metre. Such is the smallness of these nanoparticles. For example, water consists of hydrogen atoms and oxygen atoms. Two atoms of hydrogen atoms combine with one atom of oxygenatom to form one water molecule (H2O). Similarly nature has perfected the science of manufacturing matter with the help of molecules. For instance, our bodies are assembled in a specific manner from millions of living cells. Cells are nature’s nanomachines.

Some of the applications of nanotechnology have already startled us. There is a substance called sunscreen lotion. It is applied on the skin to prevent it from being burnt by the sun. Now-a-days, this substance is manufactured by using nanoparticles. With the result, no tinge is formed on the skin. Previously it was not so. As soon as the sunscreen lotion was applied on the skin, a white tinge formed on the skin. Similarly, a new type of glass is manufactured using nano-technology called active glass. These glasses have the quality of polarising ultraviolet rays i.e., arranging ultra-violet rays in a defi nite direction. This has enabled many people working with computers to protect their eyes from some harmful rays. Nanotechnology is also used in cloth manufacturing. Some clothes are coated with nanoparticles of zinc oxide. They protect the clothes from getting stained. Some clothes have nanoparticles in the form of little hairs or whiskers that help repel water and other materials making the clothing stain-resistant.

You might have seen heat-resistant and water-resistant watches. They are called so because they do not allow heat to disturb their function or water to enter the watch. Similarly we have scratch-resistant glasses. The glass is coated with aluminium silicate nanoparticles that preventthe surfaces from chipping and scratching. Many of the glass surfaces in the car belong to this category.

You might have observed doctors dressing the wounds. What they do is, they apply medicine and bandage the wounds using cotton clothes. Now-a-days they are using bandage clothes coated with nanoparticles of silver. It is enough if these clothes are wound over the wounds. They kill the harmful germs and help heal the wounds.

Nanotechnology has affected the fi eld of sports and games. For example, tennis rackets and tennis balls are made of nanomaterials. Tennis rackets made of nanomaterials mixed with graphite are lighter but at the same time stronger than steel rackets. Similarly producing tennis balls using nanotechnology is of best quality. Likewise swimming pools are cleaned and made germfree using nanotechnology cleaners.

15. Contoh report text yang singkat dalam bahasa inggris


Types of Volcanic Eruptions
Volcanic eruptions have caused some of the worst disasters in the world. They can wipe out entire cities and kill thousands of people.
The name of volcano comes from Roman term. It derives from VULCAN which is the name of Roman fire god. Romans believed that Vulcan lived on a volcanic Italian coast. Romans called the island VULCANO.

According to scientists, volcanic eruptions are divided in to four basic groups. They are commonly known as Hawaiian, Strombolian, Vulcanian and Peleean. The term of Hawaiian eruptions are named after the volcanoes in Hawaii. These volcanic eruptions are the least violent type. They produce highly fluid lava which flows quietly. This gradually builds up a shield volcano.

Strombolian eruptions are named after Stromboli. These result from the constant release of gas from the magma. As the gas escapes, it produces tephra that piles up, turning into a cinder cone. Strombolian eruptions happen when sticky magma plugs the central vent. This makes the magmatic gas build up pressure until it blasts. The magma is turned into volcanic dust and bombs.



Vulcanian eruption which comes from the ancient Roman belief, are more violent than the strombolian eruption. Vulcanian eruption happens and brings magma which is more viscous. Vulcanian explosions are usually larger and noisier than the Strombolian eruptions.



Paleean eruptions are famous as the most violent kind of volcanic eruptions. The name of paleean comes from the eruption of Mount Pelee, Martinique in 1902. It killed almost 38 thousands people. A Peleean eruption occurs when the magmatic gas build up tremendous pressure. This causes violent explosions with glowing clouds of hot ash and dust.

16. jelaskan apa itu report text dalam bahasa inggris


1. Definisi dan fungsi report text.

“Report is a text which presents information about something, as it is. It is as a result of systematic observation and analyses.” (Report adalah sebuah teks yang menghadirkan informasi tentang suatu hal secara apa adanya. Teks ini adalah sebagai hasil dari observasi dan analisa secara sistematis.)

Report text adalah suatu jenis teks yang mengupas suatu hasil pengamatan, penelitian, observasi, atau studi tentang benda, binatang, orang, atau suatu tempat. Adapaun fungsi atau tujuan dari report text itu sendiri adalah untuk menggambarkan objek apa adanya

Report Text juga dikenal dengan informational report, yang secara bahasa memiliki fungsi untuk memberikan informasi tentang suatu peristiwa atau situasi, setelah diadakannya investigasi dan melalui berbagai pertimbangan. Intinya, di dalam Report Text biasanya berisikan fakta-fakta yang dapat dibuktikan secara ilmiah.

19. Report text rafflesia arnoldi dalam bahasa inggris


Rafflesia Arnoldi.

Rafflesia Arnoldi is known as the biggest flower in the world. The name is derived from the British governor General, Sir Thomas Stamford Rafflesia , who gave attention to the botanical garden in Bogor.The Rafflesia Arnoldi consists of two parts: the stick-like part which grows in the middle and the petals around and below it. It is unusual flower because of its large size. The flower can be 1 meter in diameter and 1.40 meters in height.The Rafflesia plant begins to flower in its tenth year. It blooms three or four times a year. Before it begins to flower, the leaves and the stem become dry and look dead, but the main root in the ground is still alive. When it blooms, it has an unpleasant smell which attract insect. But when they touch the bottom part, they die.
