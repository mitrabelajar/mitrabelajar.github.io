English.

Penjelasan:

Should

Should digunakan untuk memberikan saran (suggestions). Yang namanya saran sifatnya tidak wajib berbeda dengan obligation/kewajiban/keharusan.

Pattern/pola:

S + should + V1

S + Should  be + Adjective/noun/adverb

Contoh:

Shinta should drink medicine to get well soon (Shinta sebaiknya minum obat supaya cepat sembuh) Gofar should be calm in the classroom Iman should not eat sweet and hot food. You should bring your dictionary. English is the third  lesson. You should not leave her alone. Should Gama do the homework? Yes, he should./ No, he should not.  (Apakah Gama sebaiknya mengerjakan PR? Ya./ Tidak.) Should we go to the beach? Yes, we should. No, we should not.Pelajari Lebih Lanjuthttps://brainly.co.id/tugas/17111969Detil Jawaban
