Penjelasan:

IF + PRESENT SIMPLE, ...+ IMPERATIVE

We use the imperative to give instructions of advice.

If you see John, tell him he needs to come to my office.

If you travel abroad, remember your passport

If you go to Australia, send me a postcard.

Jawaban:

Conditionals:

1. You have to learn

2. She has to eat and (has to) do exercise regularly

3. Stop

4. will not do any demonstration

5. We have to obey

6. Submit

7. will open

8. has to go to the doctor

9. Get

10. have to report

11. Why don't you tell your teacher

12. Tell me directly

13. Water boils perfectly

14. it will get darker

15. will limit

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Conditionals Sentences (kalimat pengandaian) tipe ke 1 dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat pengandaian tipe 1

if+ subject + VI (Simple Present Tense) + Subject + will + Verb-1

atau

If+ subject + (be) present + adjective/noun + Subject + will be + adjective/noun

Contoh :

If I work hard, I will get a lot of money.

If I have a lot of money, I will buy an expensive car.

9. tuliskan 5 contoh if clause + suggestion


Ini jawbannya

1. If clause + Suggestion

- If you want to make a picnic why don't you go to the travel agency to arrange is?

or

- If you want to make a picnic you should go to the travel agency to arrange this.

2. If clause + General Truth is

- If you don't water your flower, they will die
( in General truth we use the fact about something )

3. If clause + Imperative

- If you always make a date with your girl friend, get merried soon!
( imperative is " perintah " )

4. If clause + Show a dream

- If I'am a rich man, I will help the poor
( show your dream or your friends and etc )

5. If clause + reminder

- If you want fly Jakarta by plane, you have to reserved your ticket
( reminder = pengingat )
