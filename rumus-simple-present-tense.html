15. rumus simple present tense​


Jawaban:

Discussion: The formula for the simple present tense is Subject + V1 (s / es) + object. Subject He / She / It, positive sentence verbs followed by the affix s / es.

Verb 1, atau V1, memiliki nama lain yaitu infinitive. ... Singkatnya, verb 1 itu verb yang masih asli, masih berbentuk kata dasar (base form). Verb 1 umumnya hanya digunakan untuk simple present tense, dengan tambahan “s/es” jika subjectnya “he, she, it”.

16. Rumus ; -Simple present tense ; -simple continous tense ; -Simple past tense ; -Simple fiture ;


Simple present tense
(+) S + V1 (es/s)
(-) S + Do/Does + not + V1
(?) Do/Does + S + V1 ?

Simple continuous tense
(+) S + tobe (is, am.are) + V1 (ing)
(-) S + tobe + not + V1(ing)
(?) tobe + S + V1 (ing) ?
Past tense
(+) S + V2
(-) S + did not + V1
(?) Did + S + V1?

Simple future
(+) S + will/shall + V1
(-) S + will/shall + not + V1
(?) will/shall + S + V1 ?Simple Present: S+V1+O
Present Continuous: S+To Be(is, am, are)+V1ing+O
Present Perfect: S+have/has+V3+O
Present Perfect Continuous: S+have/has+been+V1ing+O

Simple Past: S+V2+O
Past Continuous: S+To Be(was, were)+V1ing+O
Past Perfect: S+had+V3+O
Past Perfect Continuous: S+had+been+V1ing+O

Simple Future: S+shall/will+V1+O
Future Continuous: S+shall/will+be+V1ing+O
Future Perfect: S+shall/will+have+V3+O
Future Perfect Continuous: S+shall/will+have+been+V1ing+O

Note:
shall= I, we
will=you,they,he,she,it
was=I,he,she,it
were=you,they,we
am=I
is=he,she,it
are=you,we,they

20. rumus simple present tense (+),(-),(?)​


Jawaban:

1. kalimat verbal

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + Object/Complement

I study English.

(-) S + do/does+ not + V1 + O/C

I do not study English.

(?) Do/does + S + V1?

Do I study English?

2. Kalimat nominal

(+) S + tobe (am/is/are) + Object/Complement

She is a teacher.

(-) S + tobe (am/is/are) + not + O/C

She is not a teacher.

(?) tobe (am/is/are)  + S + O/C?

Is she a teacher?
