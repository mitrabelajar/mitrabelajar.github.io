12. write the formula simple present continuous and simple perfect tense​


Jawaban:

kalimat positif (+)

S + be (am/is/are) + present participle (-ing)

The ships are sailing.

(Kapal-kapal itu sedang berlayar.)

He is smiling.

(Dia sedang tersenyum.)

kalimat negatif (-)

S + be (am/is/are) + not + present participle (-ing)

The ships are not sailing.

(Kapal-kapal itu tidak sedang berlayar.)

He is not smiling.

(Dia tidak sedang tersenyum.)

kalimat interogatif (?)

be (am/is/are) + S + present participle (-ing)?

Are the ships sailing?

(Apakah kapal-kapal itu sedang berlayar?)

Is he smiling?

(Apakah dia sedang tersenyum?)

15. 1. Apa simple present tense itu?2. Kapan kita menggunakan simpel present tense?3. Bagaimana formula atau rumus simple present tense?Berikan masing" satu contoh kalimat simple present tense untuk kalimat positif, negatif dan interogatif! ​


1. Simple present tense adalah bentuk kata kerja untuk menyatakan fakta, kebiasaan, atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini.

2. Simple present tense digunakan saat:

a. Menyatakan seberapa sering suatu kegiatan terjadi (frekuensi) atau habitual action (kebiasaan).

b. Menyatakan factual (kebenaran umum/fakta yang tak terbantahkan).

c. Menyatakan perasaan (feeling), indera (sense), pikiran (mental state), atau kepemilikan (possession).

d. Memberikan instruksi atau serial aksi.

e. Menyatakan suatu kondisi.

f. Membicarakan rencana atau jadwal di masa depan namun memiliki jangka waktu dekat dengan sekarang.

3. Formula simple present tense:

(+) Kalimat positif

S + verb-1/be (am/is/are)

(-) Kalimat negatif

S + auxiliary (do/does) + not + bare infinitive

S + auxiliary (do/does) + not + bare infinitiveS + be (am/is/are) + not

(?) Kalimat interogatif

do/does + S + bare infinitive

do/does + S + bare infinitivebe (am/is/are) + S

Contoh kalimat simple present tense:

(+) Kalimat positif

S + verb-1/be (am/is/are)

-She likes eating out.

-The children are naughty.

(-) Kalimat negatif

S + auxiliary (do/does) + not + bare infinitive

S + auxiliary (do/does) + not + bare infinitiveS + be (am/is/are) + not

-She doesn’t like eating out.

-The children aren’t naughty.

(?) Kalimat interogatif

do/does + S + bare infinitive

do/does + S + bare infinitivebe (am/is/are) + S

-Does she like eating out?

-Are the children naughty?

19. write the formula (rumus) of present simple and present continuous tense​


Rumus simple present

positif: S + infinitive (V1) (+s/es) / to be (is, am, are) + O.

present continuous tense

positif. Subjek + be (am/is/are) + verb (-ing) + …

(We are studying today)

ngtif. Subjek +be (am/is/are) + not + verb (-ing) +...

(She is not sleeping right now)

Interrogatif. Be (am/is/are) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?

(Are we studying today?)
