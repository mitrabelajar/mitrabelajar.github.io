8. Contoh kalimat Because , Because Of , dan Due to masing-masing 5 dan artinya


Answer: 

The word 'because', 'because of' and 'due to' are the clause of reason. The use of those three connectors are slightly different. 
'Because' is followed by phrase or sentence.
'Because of' and 'due to' are followed by noun or gerund. 

'Because'
1. The car is broken because the engine has been old. 
(mobilnya rusak karena mesinnya sudah tua)
2. He played well because he was in his best condition. 
(Dia bermain dengan baik karena dia dalam kondisi terbaik)
3. The book is gone because someone stole it. 
(Buku itu hilang karena ada yang mencurinya)
4. I can't swim because I'm afraid of water. 
 (Saya tidak bisa berenang karena saya takut air.)
5. The teacher will delay the test because there is an accident. 
 (Guru akan menunda tesnya karena ada kecelakaan)

Because of 
1. The match is delayed because of the weather. 
(pertandingan ditunda disebabkan oleh cuaca)
2. The boss holds the meeting soon because of the emergency situation.
(Sang boss mengadakan pertemuan segera karena situasi darurat)
3. I have to come early because of the meeting.
(saya harus datang lebih awal karena ada rapat)
4. The girl is sad because of losing her friend. 
(Gadis itu sedih karena kehilangan temannya)
5. He succeeded in finishing the project because of his good plan. 
(Dia berhasil menyelesaikan proyeknya karena perencanaan baiknya)

Due to 
1. They respect him due to his achievements.
(mereka menghormatinya karena prestasinya)
2. I salute him due to his patience.
(saya menganguminya karena kesabarannya)
3. The team lost the game due to his goalie's blunder. 
(tim itu kalah dalam pertandingan karena kesalahan kipernya)
4. He was lucky to be with Tanya due to his wealth.
(Dia beruntung bisa bersama Tanya karena kekayaannya)
5. The man was killed due to the accident. 
(seorang pria terbunuh karena kecelakaan)

Kelas: SMP
Mapel: English
Kategori: clause of reason

19. contoh kalimat menggunakan kata because dan because of (masing-masing 5) ​


JAWAB:

Cause and Effect

contoh kalimat menggunakan kata because dan because of (masing-masing 5)

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Because of + noun Because + subject + verb

Because

I can't do this because I don't understand how to do it.She didn't come to the party last night because her mum forbade her to come.I get a punishment from my teacher because I don't do my homeworkShe went to the dentist because she got toothache.We decided to cancel our trip because we didn't have enough money.

Because of

Because of his hard work, he managed to get the best student awardShe becomes a very famous artist because of her beauty The flight was cancelled because of the bad weatherBecause of your love, I can feel happiness.Because of his help, my father can run his business well

PEMBAHASAN:

Pengertian Cause and Effect

Cause adalah:

The reason or motive, for an action Why something happen Happen first

Effect adalah:

Result or conclusion of an action What happened Happens due to a cause

Jadi cause and effect biasa juga disebut dengan kalimat sebab-akibat atau kalimat kausalitas adalah kalimat yang dibangun dengan melibatkan tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi dan hasil dari tindakan tersebut. Paling gampang ditandai dengan kata hubung yang menandakan sebab akibat.

Signal words of cause

Ketika kita bicara tentang cause maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

Because  the reason for  On account of  Bring about  Give rise to  Created by  Contributed to  Led to  Due to  For this reason  Unless

Signal words of Effect

Ketika kita bicara tentang effect maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

As a result  Then  Hence  For this reason Outcome  So  Consequently  Finally  Therefore  In order to

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb
