How to Make 'Sambal Tempe' Tools: - Knives - Wajan - Piring - Spatula - Cutting Board - Blender - Stoves Ingredients: - Tempe - Peanuts - Anchovy - Salt - Cooking Oil - Tomatoes - Shallots and garlic - Sugar - Chili How to make: 1. Prepare all tools and ingredients 2. Slice tempeh according to taste 3. Clean all ingredients that will be used. Peel the onions and garlic, ginger and galangal and peanuts, anchovy and tomatoes and wash thoroughly. 4. Prepare a skillet that has been given oil then heat it on the stove. After the hot oil has been put into the sliced ​​tempeh. Fry the tempeh until cooked until it turns brownish yellow. Then lift and drain. 5. After frying tempeh, fry also peanuts and anchovy separately. Fry peanuts until the color turns brown. Then lift and drain. Do the same with anchovy. 6. Prepare ingredients for chili sauce. Chillies, tomatoes, onions and garlic. Put the ingredients in a blender. Add a little salt. Blend until smooth. 7. Fry the raw chilli ingredients in a skillet with enough cooking oil. 8. After the sambal is cooked, put the fried tempeh into the pan containing the chili sauce. Add a little sugar if you want the sweet taste in the sauce. Stir until tempeh and sambal blend together. 9. Lift the tempeh sauce that has been finished. 10. Then serve it on the prepared plate. Sambal tempe can be enjoyed now.
