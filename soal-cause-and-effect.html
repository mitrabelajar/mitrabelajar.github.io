3. contoh cause effect and effect cause​


Jawaban :

Contoh 1

I was lost so I opened Google Maps (Saya tersesat sehingga saya membuka Google Maps)

Cause : I was lost

Effect : I opened Google Maps

Conjunction : so

Contoh 2

The journey was quite quick because the road was clear. (perjalanan cukup cepat karena jalanan bersih dari hambatan )

Cause : the road was clear.

Effect : the journey was quite quick

Conjunction : because

Contoh 3

As it is raining again, I will have to stay at home. (karena hujan sedang turun lagi, saya akan tinggal di rumah)

Cause : it is raining again.

Effect : I will have to stay at home.

Conjunction : as

Contoh 4

Since she had not paid the rent,she was told to vacate the flat. (Karena dia belum membayar sewa, dia diberitahu untuk mengosongkan ruangan.)

Cause : she had not paid the rent.

Effect : she was told to vacate the flat.

Conjunction : since

Contoh 5

When the ocean is extremely polluted, coral reefs die..

Cause : the ocean is extremely polluted.

Effect : coral reefs die.

Conjunction : when

Hal yang perlu diperhatikan adalah jika kata hubung terletak di awal kalimat, kita perlu menggunakan tanda baca koma. Akan tetapi, jika kata hubungnya berada di tengah, kita tidak perlu menggunakan koma.

As it was getting late, We decided to book into a hotel.

(Karena sudah larut, kami memutuskan untuk memesan hotel.)

You can go first as you're the oldest.

(Anda boleh duluan karena Anda yang tertua.)

13. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang cause and effect


Cause adalah segala sesuatu yang membuat sesuatu yang lain terjadi. Sedangkan effect adalah hasil dari "cause" tersebut. Berikut ini merupakan contoh-contoh dari soal dan jawaban tentang cause dan effect.

Pembahasan:

1. Jake was ill last night, ...... he did not eat anything a whole day.

a. Because

b. Above

c. Sure

d. Sadness

2. I love Switzerland ...... the weather.

a. Because

b. As

c. Because of

d. Since

3. The increase of the cars on the road resulted ..... a more traffic jams.

a. to

b. on

c. in

d. for

4. Victor finished his job earlier due .... bad weather.

a. to

b. in

c. for

d. by

5. She will stay at home ...... she does not have anything to do outside.

a. since

b. act

c. short

d. for

6. Which one is not correct? "He did not attend the exam......, he failed."

a. As a reason

b. As a consequence

c. As a result

d. Consequently

7. We come to the school late ........ we missed the first bus.

a. whereas

b. now and then

c. due to the fact that

c. so that

8. They can win the competition ..... they always have a very hard exercise everyday.

a. Act

b. As

c. For

d. Whereas

9. I can not go to my teacher's house ..... she will have another appointment.

a. Because

b. Because of

c. Of

d. On

10. I can not eat the seafood....... my allergy.

a. because

b. because of

c. as

d. since

The answers:

1. a .     6. a

2. c .     7. c

3. c .     8. b

4. a .     9. a

5. a .     10. b
