Contoh Recount Text tentang Pertemuan

It was Friday again, the day for famous parties at Wellington High School. Students were dancing in the garden and everyone was enjoying the pleasure of being away from boring lessons.

When Sarah came to the party, the music had already started. Tim suddenly noticed Sarah and felt shocked. He had been looking for her for 2 years. They met each other at a holiday camp in 1999, but mysteriously Sarah disappeared after a while. He had been thinking about her since then.

At the party, Sarah was talking to one of her friends while most of the students were drinking as much as they could. Tim decided to talk to her and waited for her friend to leave. When he went next to her, she was searching for something in her bag, so she did not see him. As soon as he touched her on the shoulder, she raised her head and couldn’t believe her eyes. She had never forgot him.

At the end of the party, no one was happier than Sarah and Tim as they found each other after 2 years.

Terjemahan:

Hari itu hari Jumat lagi, hari pesta terkenal di Sekolah Menengah Wellington. Para siswa menari di taman dan semua orang menikmati kesenangan berada jauh dari pelajaran yang membosankan.

Saat Sarah datang ke pesta, musik sudah dimulai. Tim tiba-tiba melihat Sarah dan merasa terkejut. Dia telah mencarinya selama 2 tahun. Mereka bertemu satu sama lain di sebuah kamp liburan pada tahun 1999, tetapi secara misterius Sarah menghilang setelah beberapa saat. Dia telah memikirkannya sejak saat itu.

Di pesta itu, Sarah sedang berbicara dengan salah satu temannya sementara sebagian besar siswa minum sebanyak yang mereka bisa. Tim memutuskan untuk berbicara dengannya dan menunggu temannya pergi. Ketika dia pergi ke sampingnya, dia sedang mencari sesuatu di tasnya, jadi dia tidak melihatnya. Begitu dia menyentuh bahunya, dia mengangkat kepalanya dan tidak bisa mempercayai matanya. Dia tidak pernah melupakannya.

Di akhir pesta, tidak ada yang lebih bahagia dari Sarah dan Tim karena mereka bertemu setelah 2 tahun.

10. contoh recount text tentang virus Corona dalam bahasa Inggris​


A recount text is the kind of text which tells about someone's experience, normally personal one in thr past. Thus, the tense used in this text is written in past tense and its variation such as past contious or past perfect one.

Penjelasan:

Here is the example of a recount text about corona virus

'This year was supposed to be my final year in Juventus Calcio College in Turin, when I would be given the opportunity to work alongside the training team of Juventus U-21 plus the internship under Maurizio Sarri, the head coach of Juventus. I had been looking forward to it before suddenly the covid-19 spread in the North of Italy. That is where I have been studying and living so far for the past one year. Such news has affected all my plans and even me and my classmate have to be quarantined for 14 days in the dormitory since we were in the same place as one of Juventus' player, Daniel Rugani, was infected. During that time, the medical staffs from Ravanelli Hospital nearby went to give rapid test to all of us one by one. Luckily, the result was negative so I was thinking of going back to Surabaya first before the lockdown plan was imposed.

I had bought the ticket and prepared myself for going home. Unfortunately, the lockdown started earlier than the previous plan as the situation worsened with more and more people got infected. It definitely disappointed me though I got a full refund for my plane ticket. So, here I am now, stuck in Turin during the Ramadhan. Fortunately, the local government has never been late to deliver supplies to us in the dormitory. We even received some donation from Juventus football club, which stalls any salary payment to all of their players and coaching staffs.'

16. Contoh recount text singkat dalam bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

I spent my last summer holiday in Seoul, South Korea. I went there with my friends

Terjemahan:

Aku pergi ke Seoul, Korea Selatan untuk menghabiskan liburan musim panasku. Aku pergi ke sana bersama teman-temanku.

Penjelasan:

Apa itu recount text? Recount text bertujuan untuk menceritakan kepada pembaca mengenai kejadian atau peristiwa yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau. Karena sudah terjadi di masa lampau, maka tenses yang digunakan adalah past tense. Kamu bisa menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, past perfect, dan past perfect continuous. Struktur pembuatan recount text ada tiga yaitu orientation (pengenalan), event (peristiwa), dan reorientation (kesimpulan). Recount text biasanya bercerita tentang pengalaman pribadi.

18. Contoh cerita recount text tentang pengalaman belajar bahasa inggris


Kelas : VII
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : recount text
Kata kunci : writer's experience, past tense

Pembahasan:
Recount text is a text which retells about writer's past experience. It usually uses past tense (verb2).

Example of recount text about studying English experience.
I studied English first time when I was in junior high school. (Saya belajar bahasa Inggris pertama kali ketika saya di SMP). I never knew about English before. (Saya tidak pernah tahu tentang bahasa Inggris sebelumnya) So, I was very excited to study English. (Sehingga, saya sangat tertarik untuk belajar bahasa Inggris).

But, suddenly I hated English very much because of the English teacher. (Tetapi, tiba-tiba saya sangat membenci bahasa Inggris karena guru bahasa Inggrisnya) He taught us very discipline. (Beliau mengajar kami dengan sangat disiplin). If we said one wrong word, he would ask us to repeat it again and again. (Jika kami mengucapkan satu kata yang salah, beliau akan meminta kami mengulanginya berulang kali). If we answered wrong question, he would give us another exercises. (Jika kami menjawab pertanyaan dengan salah, beliau akan memberikan kami soal-soal yang lain). I was really afraid to come to his class. (Saya sangat takut untuk datang ke kelas beliau).

But, I never gave up. (Tetapi, saya tidak pernah menyerah). I tried to study it myself. (Saya mencoba mempelajarinya sendiri). I repeated the pronounciation and did his homework carefully. (Saya mengulangi pengucapan dan mengerjakan PR-nya dengan hati-hati). And he wasn't angry anymore. (Dan beliau tidak marah lagi). He gave me compliments. (Beliau memberi saya pujian-pujian). After that, I know English is easy to study. (Setelah itu, saya tahu bahasa Inggris mudah untuk dipelajari). So, I study it seriously and happily. (Jadi, saya mempelajarinya dengan serius dan senang).
