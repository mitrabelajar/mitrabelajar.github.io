10. buat 10 kalimat tentang asking and offering help


Berikut adalah 10 contoh kalimat asking for and offering help:

Offering: Would you like me to help you bring those?Asking: Can you give us a ride?Offering: Shall we bring the food for the party tonight?Asking: Could you tell me his phone number?Offering: May I help you carry these bags?Asking: Would you mind cleaning the room?Offering: What can we do for you?Asking: Can you help me?Offering: Should I help you?Asking: May I borrow your dress?

Pembahasan

Asking for help digunakan untuk bertanya atau meminta bantuan dalam bahasa Inggris, sedangkan offering help merupakan kalimat yang menawarkan bantuan. Untuk meminta atau menawarkan bantuan, dapat menggunakan kata kerja bantu (modal auxiliary verbs) yaitu would, shall, should, can, could, dan may.
