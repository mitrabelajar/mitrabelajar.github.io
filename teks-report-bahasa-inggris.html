4. Buatlah sebuah teks report dalam bahasa Inggris!​


JAWABAN :

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, and they’re one of the most unique-looking animals, too. With their characteristic long noses, or trunks; large, floppy ears; and wide, thick legs, there is no other animal with a similar physique.

Most experts recognize two species of elephant: the Asian elephant (Elephas maximus) and the African elephant (Loxodonta africana), who live on separate continents and have many unique features. There are several subspecies that belong to one or the other of these two main species, though experts argue over how many subspecies there are and whether or not they constitute separate species.

African elephants live in sub-Saharan Africa, the rainforests of Central and West Africa and the Sahel desert in Mali, according to National Geographic. Asian elephants live in Nepal, India and Southeast Asia in scrub forests and rainforests.

Horse is herbivorous mammals. It consists of the single species Equus caballus, which has several variations known as breeds. The horse was frequently utilized as a draft animal before the invention of modern transportation, and  horseback riding was one of the prime modes of transportation. The link between horses and people has always been special. A buddy and partner, the horse. It has plowed fields and brought in the harvest, hauled supplies and transported people, followed prey and located herds, and transported warriors into conflict and intrepid travelers to far regions. It has offered entertainment in the form of jousting, competitions, carousels, and the riding sport.

13. perbedaan teks report dan deskriptif dalam bahasa inggris


teks report adalah teks yang mendeskripsikan dan mengklasifikasikan hal hal atau fenomena yang umum yang menjadi objek diskusi, biasanya teks report di tulis berdasarkan hasil observasi atau pengamatan yang dilakukan sebelumnya, sedangkan teks deskriptif adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan atau menggambarkan bagaimana seseorang atau suatu hal yang lebih khusus. deskriptif teks menggambarkan seseorang atau suatu benda berdasarkan fakta objektif.

exp. teks report mendeskripsikan tentang kucing, maka teks report akan mendeskripsikan kucing secara general misal, makanan, cara reproduksi, dan hal umum lainnya. sedangkan teks deskriptif mendeskripsikan tentang hal hal khususnya, misal warnanya, beratnya, kebiasaannya, dan hal hal unik khusus lainnya.
