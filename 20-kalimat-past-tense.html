3. 20 contoh kalimat present tense yang diubah ke past tense


kelas : X
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : present tense vs past tense
kata kunci : present verb, past verb, nominal, verbal

pembahasan:
present tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kebenaran, umum, suatu kebiasaan, atau fakta.
rumus yang digunakan:
1) nominal present tense (tidak membutuhkan kata kerja)
subject + auxiliary verb (am/is/are) + noun
2) verbal present tense (membutuhkan kata kerja)
subject + v1 

past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang berlangsung di waktu lampau dan telah selesai.
rumus yang digunakan:
1) nominal past tense
subject + auxiliary verb (was/were) + noun
2) verbal past tense
subject + v2 (past verb)

yang membedakan antara present tense dan past tense adalah adverb, baik adverb of time maupun adverb of frequency.

contoh kalimat dari present tense (pe) menjadi past tense (pa)
1) pe = I am a student.
pa = I was a student two years ago.

2) pe = She writes a letter every month.
pa = She wrote a letter last week.

3) pe = He plays football every Sunday afternoon.
pa = He played football last Sunday afternoon.

4) pe = The students study biology in the laboratory.
pa = The students studied biology in the laboratory this morning.

5) pe = They are my classmates.
pa = They were my classmates five years ago.

6) pe = The baby drinks a bottle of milk.
pa = The baby drank a bottle of milk an hour ago.

7) pe = My mother prepares the breakfast every morning.
pa = My mother prepared the breakfast this morning.

8) pe = He cleans his room every day.
pa = He cleaned his room a week ago.

9) pe = We always go to the library after school.
pa = We went to the library this afternoon.

10) pe = My sister usually does her homework after having dinner.
pa = My sister did her homework yesterday.

11) pe = We don't eat the fruits every day.
pa = We didn't eat the fruits this evening.

12) pe = She doesn't wash her clothes regularly.
pa = She didn't wash her clothes yesterday.

13) pe = Does my father read the newspaper before going to the office?
pa = Did my father read the newspaper two hours ago?

14) pe = Do my cousins visit every school holiday?
pa = Did my cousins visit last school holiday?

15) pe = Is your uncle a postman?
pa = Was your a postman last year?

16) pe = Are they your teacher?
pa = Were they your teacher when you were in the elementary school?

17) pe = We sometimes swim at the river.
pa = We swam at the river last week.

18) pe = She sleeps on the sofa every day.
pa = She slept on the sofa last night.

19) pe = We never come late.
pa = We came late this morning.

20) pe = My mother sweeps the floor twice a day.
pa = My mother swept the floor yesterday.

Pembahasan

Simple Past Tense adalah salah satu tense yang berguna untuk menyatakan kegiatan/aktivitas yang telah terjadi atau yang sudah berlalu (kegiatan di masa lampau). Simple Past Tense dibagi menjadi 2 sentence, yaitu:

Verbal Sentence

Verbal sentence adalah bentuk Simple Past Tense jika menggunakan kata kerja. Verbal sentence dibagi lagi menjadi 3 sentences, yaitu:

Affirmative/positive sentence (S + V2 + O/C)Negative sentence (S + did not/didn't + V1 + O/C)Interrogative sentence (Did + S + V1 + O/C + ?)

Nominal Sentence

Nominal sentence adalah bentuk Simple Past Tense, tetapi tanpa menggunakan kata kerja/verb, tetapi menggunakan adjektiva/adjective dan kata benda/noun. Nominal sentence dibagi lagi menjadi 3 sentences, yaitu:

Affirmative/positive sentence (S + was/were+ Adj/Noun) Negative sentence (S + was/were+ not + Adj/Noun) Interrogative sentence (was/were + S + Adj/Noun + ?)
