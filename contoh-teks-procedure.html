How to Make a Chocoa Milkshake

A milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage which is usually made from milk, ice cream, or iced milk, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate sauce, or fruit syrup. If you want to make a delicious milkshake by your selves, then this is the way how to make it.

The ingredients that you need to make a milkshake are:

4 cups vanilla ice cream

2 cups milk

4 teaspoons chocolate sauce

1 cup fresh or frozen fruits


Now, follow these steps to make chocoa milkshake.

First, Place milkshake glasses into the freezer. Using cold glasses will help the shake stay cool to give you time to enjoy. Leave for up to an hour if possible.

Soften ice cream. It is easier to make a shake when the ice cream is slightly soft. Take the ice cream out of the freezer a few minutes before making your shake.

After that, Place the ingredients into a blender. Mix the ice cream, milk and added chocoa into the blender. Blend on high for one minute. Don’t leave in the blender for longer or the shake will melt quickly.

Then, Pour shakes into frosty glasses. Take the glasses out of the freezer as soon as the blending is complete. Then pour the shake mixture in.

Finally, Drop fruits into the shake, then enjoy.

Enjoy your milkshake.

13. contoh teks procedure tentang cara mengoperasikan sesuatu .


Contoh teks prosedur tentang cara mengoperasikan sesuatu1. Contoh Teks Prosedur Mengoperasikan Mesin Cuci

Pada umumnya, teknologi sudah menjadi kebutuhan pokok, misalnya mesin cuci. Namun, masih banyak masyarakat yang belum paham bagaimana cara mengoperasikan mesin cuci dengan baik. berikut adalah langkah-langkah mengoperasikan mesin cuci dengan baik dan benar:

•Pertama, masukkan kabel colokan mesin cuci ke stop kontak

•Selanjutnya masukkan pakaian yang akan dicuci ke dalam tabung mesin cuci. Pisahkan terlebih dahulu pakaian yang mudah luntur dengan yang tidak mudah luntur

•Langkah ketiga tuangkan deterjen (disarankan deterjen cair) kemudian isilah tabung pencucian dengan air

•Aturlah siklus pencucian ke arah angka 10 menit. Ulangi langkah ini selama tiga kali agar pakaian bebas dari debu dan kotoran yang menempel

•Setelah turbin berhenti, buanglah air cucian tersebut dengan menekan tombol drain

•Berikutnya ambil pakaian dan pindahkan ke tabung pengering dan tutup rapat

•Putarlah tombol pengering ke arah angka 5 menit. Untuk langkah ini dilakukan satu kali saja

•Langkah yang terakhir, angkat dan jemur pakaian. Jangan lupa matikan mesin cuci.

2. Contoh Teks Prosedur Mengoperasikan Rice Cooker

Rice cooker adalah alat elektronik rumah tangga yang berguna untuk memasak nasi dengan mudah. Banyak dari kalangan masyarakat yang sudah menggunakan rice cooker. Bagi yang belum pernah menggunakan rice cooker, berikut adalah langkah mengoperasikan rice cooker untuk memasak nasi:

•Langkah pertama, bersihkan terlebih dahulu beras yang akan dimasak

•Berikutnya masukkan beras ke dalam wadah rice cooker dan tambahkan air sesuai dengan takaran yang ada pada rice cooker tersebut

•Pastikan colokan rice cooker sudah ditancapkan lalu nyalakan rice cooker dan tutup rice cooker

•Selanjutnya tekan tombol Cook pada rice cooker sampai lampu tanda Cook menyala

•Yang terakhir tunggu sampai lampu dan tombol berubah ke warm. Jika sudah, nasi sudah siap untuk disantap.

How to Use a Printer

You need:

Printer

Directions:

Step 1

Using a printer even basic printer is extremely easy to do. There are many different models of printers out there. You have your very basic to your all-in-one printer. The printer with everything on it works pretty much just like the regular printer you just have a lot more buttons to push

Step 2

Make sure that your printer is hooked up properly before using it. If this is a brand new printer then it will go through a check and print out a test page for you. A lot of different times you can just turn it on, you will hear the ink moving around. Something might come up on the computer telling you that the printer is on and working properly.

Step 3

Open the page you want to print. If you are on the Internet and want to print something from there. All you have to do is go up to file, then down to page set up. This will give you more printing options to choose from. When you are done setting up the page you can verify you have the right printer hooked up by clicking on “Printer” at the bottom. Once everything looks good click on “OK” and now your page should print out.

Step 4

Open up a “.doc” file if that is where your page is that you need to print. When you are ready to print go up to “File” and “Print Setup”. Again you can choose how you want your page to look, and check and make sure the right printer is installed. If your page is set up the way you like it you can just click on the printer icon on the tool bar or go to “File” then “Print.”

Step 5

Using a printer a lot over time will start to wear down your ink supply. Some models of printers let you know when your ink supply is getting low. If you have an older printer you will just have to go by what the printed page looks like. If it is very worn and faded then it is time to install a new ink cartridge
