diket:

motif batik(x) = 100.000

motif polos(y) = 80.000

.  =100.000x+80.000y<20.800,000          (:10.000)

  =5x+4y<1040

.   x+y<200

jadi, model matematikanya = 5x+4y<1.040; x+y<200;x>0;y>0
